My old dog, Maxie

This was my pet dog, Maxie, who I decided to base my raster project on. Unfortunately, he passed away in April 2017 at 12 years old, so I did this as a sort of dedication to him. I used song lyrics from Yuna's Lullabies because even though it's been a year since he's gone, I'll never really forget him.
