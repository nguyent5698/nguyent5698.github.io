Gill-man takes a swim

After recently seeing Guillermo Del Toro's The Shape of Water the Gill-man was on my mind as I did my vector project. It was definitely a struggle, as I spent maybe 4 hours using the pen tool trying to draw him. The pen tool is not as versatile as I hoped, so in retrospect I wish I used the brush tool instead. I also originally wanted to animate him, but considering how hard it was to draw in illustrator I wanted to come up with a new idea.
